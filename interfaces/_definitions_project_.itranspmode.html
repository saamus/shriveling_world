Interface ITranspMode

A transport mode has

  • a name,
  • a code,
  • a yearBegin,
  • a yearEnd (optionnal),
  • can be terrestrial or not,
  • and has a table of speeds that may change over years

If mode is 'terrestrial' the transport mode speed can affect the slope of cones
